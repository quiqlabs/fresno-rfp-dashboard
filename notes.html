2026-05-27 · Run #16 (FCSS Analysis)

Strategy FCSS is "no current RFPs" — but most procurement is under $175k informal-bid threshold

The FCSS Notice of RFQs page is empty most of the time. That's misleading: FCSS operates under CUPCCAC, meaning:

  • ≤ $45,000 → direct PO / negotiated, no bidding at all
  • $45k–$175k → informal bidding from a qualified-bidder list (Nov registration window)
  • > $175k → formal RFP (this is what shows on the public page)

Most Quiq Labs deals (Resource Portal pilots, Multimedia projects, Custom Dashboards) fit the under-$175k informal bracket. The CUPCCAC list registration is the single highest-leverage action.

⏰ Annual action item: November = CUPCCAC registration window. Already scheduled as the fcss-cupccac-november-reminder task.
